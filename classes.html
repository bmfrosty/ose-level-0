Smoothified (Gygar) progressions — to-hit bonus and saving throws for all classes, levels 1–14

Smoothified Mode uses gradual attack bonus and saving throw progressions instead of the original OSE big jumps. All classes advance every 1–2 levels. Demihuman level limits are removed — all classes reach level 14.

For official OSE Standard progressions, see the OSE SRD.
